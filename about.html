About me.



I’m Rahmatollah Isapanah Amlashi, but every body calls me Sadra.

I was born in Amlash at 1991/3/21. I’m living in Gorgan, about 20 years. I have educated Bachelor in Telecommunications engineering at IAUAK University. Because of my skills and job positions, I’ve decided to educate Master of in e-Commerce at IAUEC.

I started Blogging in 2005 after I bought my first computer. After a while, I moved working on social networks and I had some pages on Facebook, at the same time I was busy with working on Google Adsense.

Before 2015, I was just a mobile application developer freelancer. From 2015 till now, I am Senior iOS and Android developer at ParsFibernet. During this time we made some awesome app like Android/iOS FIbernet App, Android/iOS Foodnet App.

I started programming in 2003 using PHP and then moved on to Android and started a career coding Android mobile applications. I then got interested in Apple products and that helped shape my future in Mobile application development. Recently I improved my skill in Javascript and I’ve been interested in exploring Python, Go & Ruby as a hobby.

Maybe you were interested to know, I use Node.Js for Backend, ReactJs for Frontend and Docker for controlling web application in my personal project.
